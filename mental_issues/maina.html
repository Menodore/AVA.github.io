Mania Support

Answer the following questions to check for possible Mania symptoms in you

1. Do you experience periods of unusually high energy, feeling wired or overly excited for extended periods?

2. Do you feel like you need much less sleep than usual but still feel energetic?

3. Do you talk much faster than usual, finding it difficult for others to keep up with you?

4. Do you have racing thoughts or find your mind jumping quickly from one idea to another?

5. Do you feel an inflated sense of confidence or self-importance during these high-energy periods?

6. Do you make impulsive or risky decisions (e.g., reckless spending, unsafe sex, or gambling)

7. Do you feel easily distracted, struggling to focus on one task?

8. Do you experience extreme irritability or restlessness during these high-energy phases?

9. Do you have an uncontrollable urge to start new projects or take on excessive activities?

10. Do you feel more social, outgoing, or overly talkative compared to your usual self?

11. Do you find yourself acting without considering the consequences of your actions?

12. Have others expressed concern about your sudden changes in mood or behavior?
