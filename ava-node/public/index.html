Our Story

At Ava, we're more than just a wellness platform – we're a sisterhood. Founded by three women who understand the silent struggles that often go unspoken, we've created a space where women can truly be themselves, without fear of judgment.

As friends and co-founders, we saw the gaps in the wellness industry – spaces that felt impersonal, and tools that didn't offer the support women needed for their unique health and mental wellness journeys. We wanted to build something real. Something personal. Something that could feel like the sisterhood we always wished we had – a place where women could find not just information, but also a compassionate guide who truly understands.

Ava was born from this desire. We aim to be the support that's always there, the advice that feels like it comes from your closest friend, and the tools that can help you take charge of your wellness with confidence and ease. Whether it's tracking your period, managing PCOS, exploring mental health, or just seeking someone who gets it – we're here for you, just like a sister would be.

Because we know, sometimes the most important thing a woman can have is a sister to rely on. And that's exactly what we want to be for you.

Our Mission

At Ava, we strive to create an empowering, compassionate space that encourages women to prioritize their wellness. Our mission is to provide women with the tools and support to better understand their bodies and mind, offering personalized solutions that make wellness approachable, accessible, and meaningful.

We believe every woman deserves a sister who listens, understands, and helps guide them on their health journey, no matter where they are in life.
