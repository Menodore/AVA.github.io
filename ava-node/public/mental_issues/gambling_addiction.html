Gambling Addiction Symptom Checker

1. Do you frequently think about gambling, such as past experiences or planning your next bet?

2. Do you feel restless or irritable when trying to cut down or stop gambling?

3. Do you gamble to escape stress, anxiety, or depression?

4. Have you needed to bet increasing amounts of money to feel excited while gambling?

5. Have you lied to family or friends about how much you gamble?

6. Have you ever tried to stop gambling but found yourself unable to?

7. Do you chase losses by gambling more to try to recover lost money?

8. Has gambling caused problems in your relationships, work, or daily life?

9. Have you borrowed money, sold belongings, or taken loans to fund your gambling?

10. Do you feel guilty or regretful after gambling but continue doing it?

11. Have you missed important events or responsibilities due to gambling?

12. Have you ever felt that gambling is out of your control?
