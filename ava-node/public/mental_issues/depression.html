Depression Symptom Checker

1. Do you often feel sad, empty, or hopeless for most of the day?

2. Have you lost interest in activities that you once enjoyed?

3. Do you experience significant changes in your appetite (overeating or loss of appetite)?

4. Do you have trouble sleeping (insomnia) or sleep excessively?

5. Do you feel fatigued or lack energy even after resting?

6. Do you experience feelings of worthlessness or excessive guilt?

7. Do you have difficulty concentrating or making decisions?

8. Have you lost motivation to complete daily tasks or responsibilities?

9. Do you feel restless or unusually slow in your movements?

10. Do you struggle with feelings of detachment from others?

11. Do you experience frequent thoughts of self-harm or suicide?

12.Have you noticed increased irritability or frustration, even over small issues?
