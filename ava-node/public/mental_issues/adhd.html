ADHD Symptom Checker

Answer the following questions to check for possible ADHD symptoms in you as an adult!

1. Do you often have difficulty paying attention to details or making careless mistakes in work or daily activities?

2. Do you frequently struggle to stay focused on tasks or conversations?

3. Do you often find yourself easily distracted by external stimuli or unrelated thoughts?

4. Do you frequently forget to complete tasks, appointments, or obligations?

5. Do you often avoid or delay tasks that require sustained mental effort?

6. Do you frequently fidget, tap your hands or feet, or feel restless when seated?

7. Do you find yourself talking excessively or blurting out answers before questions are completed?

8. Do you often interrupt or intrude on conversations or activities?

9. Do you struggle with time management, frequently missing deadlines or underestimating time needed for tasks?

10. Do you often lose important items like keys, wallets, or documents?

11. Do you struggle with emotional regulation, experiencing frequent mood swings or frustration?

12. Do you find it difficult to unwind or relax when needed?
