Thyroid Symptom Checker

Answer the following questions to check for possible thyroid imbalances.

1. Unusual weight gain or difficulty losing weight

2. Fatigue, loss of energy, or sleeping more than average

3. Hair loss, brittle hair, itchy scalp, or loss of eyebrows

4. Dry skin and brittle nails

5. Difficulty tolerating temperature changes or sensitivity to cold

6. Feeling forgetful, fuzzy-minded, or absent-minded

7. Feeling irritable or having mood swings

8. Puffiness in face and extremities

9. Constipation and other bowel problems

10. Numbness or tingling in the hands

11. Diminished sex drive or other sexual dysfunction

12. Body aches (e.g., headache, joint aches, muscle aches)
