Eating Disorder Symptom Checker

Assess potential symptoms of eating disorders and find support

Important: This is not a diagnostic tool. If you're struggling with eating behaviors, please contact the National Eating Disorders Association Helpline at 1-800-931-2237 for support.

1. Do you frequently feel guilty or ashamed after eating?

2. Do you often avoid eating in public or around others?

3. Do you engage in strict dieting, fasting, or excessive exercise to control your weight?

4. Do you experience intense fear of gaining weight, even when at a healthy or low weight?

5. Do you frequently binge eat (eating large amounts of food in a short period, feeling out of control)?

6. Do you use purging behaviors, such as vomiting or using laxatives, to control your weight?

7. Do you obsess over calorie counting, food tracking, or weight control?

8. Do you feel that your self-worth is largely based on your weight or body shape?

9. Do you experience dizziness, fainting, or fatigue due to extreme eating habits?

10. Do you often compare your body to others and feel dissatisfied?

11. Do you eat when feeling emotional (e.g., stress, sadness, or boredom) rather than hunger?

12. Have you noticed significant fluctuations in your weight due to eating habits?
